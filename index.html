angel-nn

angel-nn is a neural network that you can feed, train and get predictions about Mariah Carey whistle register notes

Guide:

  • Click anywhere on Mariah to hear a whistle note
  • Press any key between 1 and 9 to change notes
  • Click on different places using different notes to give angel-nn some varied training data
  • When you are ready, press the 't' key and wait for angel-nn to learn Mariah's notes
  • Now when you click on Mariah you don't need to select a 1-9 number, click anywhere, angel-nn will predict what note should play based on where you clicked the training data

Notes:

  • angel-nn currently only works with chromium browsers
  • The click-noises during training data are cut from a Mariah Carey recording. The predicted notes after training are generated using the p5.js sound library by matching frequencies
